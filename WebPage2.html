Treasure Hunting Game

Background Story

After your latest skydiving adventure, you happened to land on an island unknown to anyone on the plane. The plane flew on, leaving you on this island by yourself. To get back home, explore the island in search for the buried treasure. Once you find this, you will be able to offer it to the island inhabitants in return for a trip home. Interact with them to get hints on where to go and advice on how to get there. Beware of the animals in the jungle- make sure you have protection! Good luck!

How to Play the Game

Read the screen and press the buttons that describe which choice you want to make. If an alert message comes up, follow the instructions to unlock the choice you wanted to make. If you need help during the game, find the map and use it for tips.
